Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasgna is an Italian dish. It is made by pasta, ragu, tomato and cheese.

It is very easy to prepare, here you can see the ingredients and steps.

Ingredients

  • Pasta in lasagna format
  • Ragu style meat
  • Fried tomato
  • Bechamel
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the meat in a pan
  2. Mix the meat with the tomato
  3. Prepare the pasta following the instructions on the package
  4. In a plate for the oven, put a piece of pasta, then the meat on the top
  5. Repeat step 4 until we finish the meat
  6. In the last layer add the bechamel and cheese
  7. Put it in the oven for 30 minutes at 180 degrees Celsius and its is ready to eat!
