Meatballs with tomato Recipe

Description

It is very easy to prepare, if you buy the meatballs prepared, but ni¡ot that much if you want to do them. Here you can see the ingredients and steps.

Ingredients

  • garlic
  • bay leaf
  • bread
  • meat
  • tomato

Steps

  1. In a large non-stick skillet over medium-high heat, lightly brown the garlic in the oil with the bay leaf and red pepper flakes. Add the tomatoes and simmer for 30 minutes. When they begin to break down, coarsely crush the tomatoes using a potato masher. Season with salt and pepper. Keep warm.
  2. Meanwhile, in a food processor, pulse the bread until the size of bread crumbs. In a large bowl, soak the bread crumbs in the milk for 5 minutes. Add the remaining ingredients and, using your hands, combine well.
  3. Working with lightly oiled hands, shape each meatball using 2 tbsp (30 ml) of the mixture.
  4. Place the meatballs in the tomato sauce, cover and cook for 10 minutes over medium-low heat, turning them a few times during cooking. Uncover and continue cooking for 10 minutes or until cooked through.
